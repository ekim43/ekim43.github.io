Top Trade Candidates

  • Phil Kessel: As the Leaf's most skilled offensive player, Phil would be a highly sought after target if the Leafs decide to blow up the team at the end of the season.
  • Joffrey Lupul: Although the oft injured player has not had a great year, Joffrey's skill level would warrant interest from a number of playoff teams.
  • Dion Phaneuf: While the captain of the Leafs has a contract that will scare most NHL teams away, a handful of teams will likely be willing to take on Dion's massive contract if the Leafs are willing to eat some of the contract.

Recent Rumors

New York Rangers, Toronto Maple Leafs Talking Mike Santorelli Trade?

TSN Insider Darren Dreger, appearing on Tuesday night's episode of Insider Trading, revealed that the Rangers are seeking a centerman and could have eyes for Toronto Maple Leafs forward Mike Santorelli. "They're (Rangers) looking for a centerman and there might be a Leaf connection here. Keep in mind they tried to sign Mike Santorelli as an unrestricted free agent," said Dreger, per NicholsOnHockey.com. "All they had was $1 million, so the Leafs got him at $1.5 million. If things continue to go poorly for Toronto, maybe Santorelli becomes a deadline deal."

Blockbuster Trade Imminent?

A recent report by www.hockeybuzz.com is an intriguing one. According to the hockey website, the Leafs are involved in serious discussions with the L.A. Kings and Colorado Avalanche. The one Kings player being mentioned most is Mike Richards, but would he help the Leafs? On the one hand, Toronto would love to have more depth and experience at the centre position.
